A bundle is a list of related collections. A collection is a list of related basic products. A basic product is the simplest product in PDS4 and there are two types, observational and non-observational products. Examples of basic products are Product_Context, Product_Document, Product_XML_Schema, and Product_Observational. This is not a complete list of PDS4 basic products; however, these products will be discussed within this tutorial. Aside from basic products, PDS4 has aggregate products. Product_Bundle and Product_Collection are aggregate products and are the only two products that are not basic products. Each bundle has exactly one Product_Bundle and each collection has exactly one Product_Collection.

Enter the name of your bundle below and select the appropriate collections that your bundle is composed of. ELSA then creates the overarching bundle directory structure and begins the Product_Bundle and Product_Collection labels within your bundle. The following steps of this tutorial then go into PDS4's basic products for the document, context, xml_schema, and data collections.

For more information, please visit PDS4 Concepts.
